How many reps at a % of your 1RM?

Bench press — pooled research data, not a single-formula estimate.

These numbers come from a 2024 meta-analysis that pooled 962 reps-to-failure tests from 7,289 people across the published literature, and modelled the average reps people actually hit at each percentage of their one-rep max. The bench press got its own table because people manage fewer reps at a given %1RM on bench than on lower-body lifts like the leg press.

The one number to remember

On bench, the average person grinds out about 14 reps at 70% of 1RM, 9 at 80%, and only 4 at 90%. Old "rep-max charts" (the ones that say 10 reps = 75%) tend to overrate how many bench reps a given load allows.
